Eligibility Criteria

  1. Course Completion

    You must:

    • Complete 100% of the course content within six months of enrolment, including all lessons, modules, assessments, and activities.

  2. Business Registration

    • You must register your business with the Australian Business Register and obtain a valid ABN (Australian Business Number).

  3. Business Launch

    Your business must be actively launched and accepting paying clients within one month of completing the course. A business is considered to have launched when:

    • You have a suitable premise that meets local council requirements for provision of beauty services, and;
    • You have launched your social media accounts, either via Instagram or TikTok.
    • You have set up a business bank account to receive funds.

  4. Ongoing Effort & Commitment

    You must commit to and demonstrate at least 5 hours of work per day, 5 days per week, over 5 consecutive months after your business launch. Acceptable forms of evidence include:

    • Time-tracking logs, schedules, or planner entries
    • Booking platform or client management system reports

  5. Revenue Tracking

    • You must provide verifiable documentation confirming that your gross business income did not reach AUD $5,000 within the 5-month period post-launch by submitting your business bank account statements in PDF format from your bank.

How to Submit a Refund Request

If, after meeting all of the above criteria, you do not reach $5,000 in revenue within 5 months of launching your business, you may request a refund. To do so, email support@australianbeautyschool.com.au within 14 days of your 5-month business milestone, including:
